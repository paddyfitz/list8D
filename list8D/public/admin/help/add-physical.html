Adding Physical Media to a list

Choose the Physical Media type from the 'Create new item' option. The 'search for resource' screen will appear.

You will now have the option to add an item as follows:

Physical Media - search by:

  • Keyword
  • Catalogue item ID

After entering the details you will then have the option to add an item to your list by clicking on the '+add to list' button. The requested item will be displayed at the bottom of your list.
