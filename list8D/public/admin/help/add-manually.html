Adding an item manually

Choose the resource type required from the 'Create new item' option. The 'search for resource' screen will appear. You will now have the option to search for an item by entering it's details.

If the item you are searching for is not available to add to your list, then you can add the details manually by selecting 'Add resource manually' from the side bar.

Having added the details manually the item will be appended to the bottom of your list and you can now edit the item as necessary by adding Author/chapter/pages, Author/vol/issue/year/pages and notes etc.

Click 'Save' to save your editing
