Adding a Journal or Article to a list

Choose the journal type from the 'Create new item' option. The 'search for resource' screen will appear.

You will now have the option to add an item as follows:

Journal - search by:

  • Entering the Journal title and the article title only. The system will search our Serials Solutions (e-journals) database and if the article is found, will insert a link directly to it. Occasionally, although we have access to the on-line article, the link will only go to the Journal front page. When an online copy is not available then the link will go to the library catalogue for hard copy holdings.
  • Entering the articles 'DOI' will ensure a definite link to the on-line article.

After entering the details you will then have the option to add an item to your list by clicking on the '+add to list' button. The requested item will be displayed at the bottom of your list.
